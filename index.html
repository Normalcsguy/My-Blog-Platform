Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water (for sugar syrup)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and baking powder.
  2. Add the yogurt and water gradually to form a smooth batter. Let it sit for about 2 hours.
  3. In a saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup of water. Heat until the sugar dissolves. Add cardamom powder and set aside.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  5. Transfer the batter to a piping bag or squeeze bottle. Pipe the batter into the hot oil in circular motions to form the Jalebi shapes.
  6. Fry until golden and crispy. Remove from the oil and immediately dip into the warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.
  7. Remove from syrup and let drain on paper towels. Serve warm or at room temperature.
